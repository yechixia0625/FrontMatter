## Recommended Demo Script

### Step 1: Login

Open the application and sign in through the shared demo gate.

### Step 2: Upload a Space Image

Upload a storefront image or interior space photo.

### Step 3: Enter Lease Inputs

Fill in:

- business type
- rent
- size
- lease term
- fit-out budget

Optionally fill in advanced assumptions such as staffing, utilities, and discount rate.

### Step 4: Select a Singapore Location

Use either:

- `At site now`
- `Search address`

Expected behavior:

- Singapore addresses return suggestions
- non-Singapore current location is rejected

### Step 5: Run Analysis

Click `Analyze Space`.

Expected output:

- spatial blueprint or live market map
- score and risk flags
- discounted cash flow metrics
- scenario analysis
- candidate comparison if provided

## What Judges Should Look For

- whether the workflow feels coherent from intake to decision output
- whether the system reflects a real Singapore lease due diligence problem
- whether the economic outputs are structured and explainable
- whether the product avoids overstating certainty

## Demo Boundaries

- this is not a legal or valuation tool
- market evidence is contextual
- nearby businesses are observations, not proof of footfall
- outcomes depend on input assumptions
